Event Guide


What to Bring:

  • Laptop
  • Wear Comfortable Shoes
  • Water

Parking:

  • There is free parking directly across from the Hyatt.
  • The Hyatt parking garage charges a fee for parking and is also available.

How to Participate:

  • Review the speaker schedule and plan your day.
  • There is a great Badge game and CTF designed so that anyone can participate.
  • Join the various CTF challenges.
  • Walk the floor.
  • Enjoy a free lunch.
  • Enjoy a free happy hour at the end.
  • Most important of all: Meet New People.

CTF:

  • Capture the Flag Competition available for anyone to participate.
  • Prizes awarded for top three spots.

Puzzle:

  • This has shifted to a Badge challenge and CTF.
  • Use the following QR code or link: OzSec 2024 Badge CTF (ctfd.io)

Vendor Passport Game:

  • During the event, visit each of the sponsor booths and get your card stamped.
  • Turn it in for the raffle at the end of the day.

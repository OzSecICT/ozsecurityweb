Get to know the panelists.

Nicki Swart

Information Security Analyst @ Heartland Credit Union | MSCIS | (ISC)2 Wichita Marketing Director | GWLN - KS Sister Society | Adjunct Professor of IT @ WSU Tech | Speaker

Nicki holds a master’s degree in computer information systems with a concentration in cybersecurity and a Bachelor of Science in Business Analytics and Management Systems. With both a business and technical background, she bridges the gap between security and organizational goals by translating complex cybersecurity concepts for diverse audiences. With experience as an Information Security Analyst in the healthcare and finance industries, Nicki specializes in GRC, security awareness, risk management, and disaster recovery. At present, she is an Information Security Analyst for Heartland Credit Union. She currently serves in leadership roles for both the Wichita ISC² chapter and the local Global Women’s Leadership Network Sister Society chapter. She is also an adjunct professor of IT at WSU Tech. Nicki is passionate about cybersecurity education and mentoring future professionals in the field.

Amanda Taylor

VP, Security and Risk @ Data Center Inc.

As the Vice President of Security and Risk at Data Center Inc., a leading technology service provider for community banks, I am responsible for driving security, resilience, and compliance across our operations. With 15+ of expertise in business resilience, disaster recovery, information security, and IT regulatory compliance, I’ve built a strong foundation in securing critical banking operations. Passionate about empowering others, I’m dedicated to fostering a culture of security awareness through advanced training programs and mentoring my team. At the core of my work is a commitment to ensuring secure, resilient, and compliant operations that support the future of banking.

Mark Rodee

Chief Information Security Officer, WSU

Stuart Yearout
